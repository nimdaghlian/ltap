What Kind of Work is Painting?





Painting is not the only discipline to say, in one way or another, that the real work is beyond the craft of the work. If you’re not a painter, but some of the tweets above rang true for the particular thing you are trying to do, then you’re familiar with this kind of work. This essay is about what that work seems to be, what it is like to do it within a discipline, and how different fields share this particular method.
